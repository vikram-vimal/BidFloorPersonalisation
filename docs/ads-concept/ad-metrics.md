# Ad Metrics

A practical glossary of mobile ad metrics used in monetization, UA, and mediation. Definitions are geared toward app publishers and ad tech teams.

## Core delivery metrics

| Metric | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Impressions** | Number of times an ad is shown on screen |
| **Ad requests** | Number of times the app asks a network or bid partner for an ad |
| **Fill rate** | Share of ad requests that return a filled ad — filled impressions ÷ requests |
| **Show rate** | Share of loaded ads actually shown to the user; commonly tracked in rewarded and interstitial flows |

## Revenue metrics

| Metric | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **CPM** | Cost per mille — price paid for 1,000 impressions |
| **eCPM** | Effective CPM — revenue per 1,000 impressions; standard for comparing formats and partners |
| **ARPDAU** | Average revenue per daily active user — revenue ÷ DAU over a day |
| **ARPU** | Average revenue per user over a defined period |
| **ARPPU** | Average revenue per paying user over a period |

## Auction and mediation metrics

| Metric | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Bid request** | Request sent to a demand partner asking for a bid |
| **Bid response** | Bid returned by a demand partner |
| **Win rate** | Percentage of bid responses that win the auction |
| **Match rate** | Share of opportunities where a user or impression can be matched to demand |
| **Take rate** | Share of value retained by a platform or exchange; meaning varies by product |
| **Floor price** | Minimum acceptable price for an impression in an auction or waterfall |
| **Latency** | Time for an ad request, auction, or load to complete |
